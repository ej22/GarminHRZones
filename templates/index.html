Configuring Zones on Your Garmin

Via Garmin Connect (Web / App)
  1. Open Garmin Connect (app or connect.garmin.com)
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon) → User Settings
  3. Select Heart Rate Zones
  4. Turn off Auto Detect to enable manual entry
  5. Enter the lower BPM boundary for each zone from the results above
  6. Tap Save — zones will sync to your device
Directly on the Watch
  1. Hold MENU (or long-press touchscreen)
  2. Navigate to Settings → User Profile → Heart Rate Zones
  3. Select Based On → choose BPM
  4. Enter the lower boundary for each zone
  5. Press Back to save
Sport-Specific Zones
  1. In Garmin Connect, go to Device Settings → Activities & Apps
  2. Select the activity (e.g., Running, Cycling)
  3. Tap Heart Rate Zones
  4. Disable Use Default Zones
  5. Enter custom boundaries for that sport
Note: Garmin uses lower-boundary-only semantics — each zone starts at the value you enter and ends where the next zone begins. Make sure Auto Detect is disabled or your custom values will be overwritten. LTHR (Lactate Threshold Heart Rate) zones are a separate Garmin feature and are not covered by this calculator.
